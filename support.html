Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with MyDrAI?

After downloading the app, you'll go through a simple onboarding process where you'll set up your profile, choose your health goals, and select your preferred AI doctor. The app will guide you through each step.

Is MyDrAI a replacement for real doctors?

No, MyDrAI is not a replacement for professional medical care. It's designed to provide health information, wellness tracking, and general guidance. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

How does the image analysis feature work?

You can upload photos through the chat interface, and our AI doctors will analyze them to provide insights. This feature uses advanced AI technology to help identify potential health concerns, but it's not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis.

Can I use MyDrAI without a subscription?

Yes! MyDrAI offers a free tier with basic features. Premium subscribers get access to advanced AI features, unlimited consultations, and priority support. You can upgrade anytime through the app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store account settings. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions, find MyDrAI, and tap "Cancel Subscription."

Is my health data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Your health information is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
