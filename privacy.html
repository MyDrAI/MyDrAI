We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information.

1. Information We Collect

MyDrAI collects the following types of information to provide you with personalized health assistance:

Personal Information:

  • Name and age (for personalized greetings)
  • Gender (for health recommendations)
  • Profile image (optional)
  • Email address (for account management)

Health Information:

  • Health concerns and symptoms you share
  • Images you upload for analysis
  • Health tracking data (habits, scores, streaks)
  • Chat conversations with AI doctors

Usage Information:

  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Feature interactions and engagement
  • Device information and technical data
  • Notification preferences and settings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized AI health advice and recommendations
  • Analyze uploaded images for health insights
  • Track your wellness journey and progress
  • Send personalized health reminders and notifications
  • Improve our AI models and app functionality
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal health information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate the app (data storage, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Emergency Situations: If we believe there's an immediate threat to your safety
  • Consent: When you explicitly give us permission

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive health data
  • Secure cloud storage with encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Secure data transmission protocols

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Until you delete your account
  • Health Data: As long as you use the app (you can request deletion)
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized data may be retained for research
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained for legal compliance

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and data collection
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions at any time

7. Third-Party Services

MyDrAI may integrate with third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For AI chat functionality (data is processed securely)
  • Apple Health: For health data integration (with your permission)
  • Analytics: For app improvement (anonymized data only)

8. Children's Privacy

MyDrAI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during international transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of MyDrAI after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
